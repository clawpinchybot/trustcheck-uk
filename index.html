What This Tool Checks

⚠️ Red Flags to Watch For

  • Phoenix companies — Director has dissolved multiple previous companies
  • Late filings — Accounts overdue suggests financial trouble
  • Dissolved status — Company no longer exists
  • No Companies House record — Sole trader (no limited company)
